High Rollers

Roll Your Way to Victory!

Instructions

  • Players can edit their names by clicking the "edit names" button
  • Set the desired winning score. Else it will be set to 50 by default
  • Click "Roll Dice" to roll the dice
  • Each player gets to play twice then pass it over
  • Click "Switch" to move to the next player
  • The sum of the dice will be the points
  • The first player to reach the winning score wins
  • Click "New Game" to restart the game
